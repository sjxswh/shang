    About H&R Block At Home

    Founded in 1955 by brothers Richard and Henry Bloch, H&R Block has blossomed from its roots as a Kansas Citybased tax-prep service into one of the most recognizable brands in business, worldwide. With a network of over 70,000 eminently trained tax professionals, H&R Block processes one out of every seven tax returns in the United States, giving its accountants a well-trained insight into how to maximize your return or minimize your payment. Since the company maintains a retail office within five miles of most Americans, expert help with your taxes is never far away, and with an H&R Block coupon code, its more accessible than ever before.

    Though the company will probably always be best known for its excellence in the tax-prep industry, H&R Block also helps its clients manage their assets outside of tax season. Through a partnership with BofI Federal Bank, customers can keep their money safe and secure, while the H&R Emerald Card can help you establish a line of credit while earning cash rewards. Whatever your financial portfolio needs, an H&R Block promo code can get you closer to the assistance you need.
